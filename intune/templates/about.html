What is InTune?

InTune is a web application that helps you discover your Spotify listening habits. Get insights into your music taste by viewing your most played artists and tracks across different time periods, creating custom playlists, and sharing your top sounds.

Built with Python Flask and powered by the Spotify Web API, InTune gives you an interface to explore your music data.

Features

Music Analytics

View your top artists, tracks, and genres across different time ranges

Popularity Metrics

See how niche your music taste is with popularity scores for your top artists and tracks

Playlist Creation

Easily create custom Spotify playlists directly from your top tracks

Social Sharing

Generate and share story images showcasing your monthly listening highlights

Technical Overview

Built With

  • Spotify Web API
  • HTML5 & CSS3
  • JavaScript

Features Include

  • Data Visualization

  • OAuth 2.0 Authentication
  • Session Management
  • Automatic Token Refreshing

Privacy & Security

InTune only requests the minimum permissions necessary to provide its features and does not store your personal data permanently. All authentication is handled securely through Spotify's OAuth 2.0 system.

No permanent storage of your data
Secure OAuth 2.0 authentication
Uses the minimum Spotify access level
